直接删除jdk目录即可

JDK所在目录为/Library/Java/JavaVirtualMachines/jdk_major.minor.macro_\[_update\].jdk

比如删除以下 jdk 1.8

# rm -rf /Library/Java/JavaVirtualMachines/jdk1.8.0_06.jdk
